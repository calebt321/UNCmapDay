Sentiments of Fisherment Towards NC Fishieries Managment

Caleb Tyler

Using data collected by UNC a team of UNC and ECU researchers of NC fishermen, opinions towards fishery managment were collected and displayed in the below maps.

Using a combination of online and mail-in surveys the following maps display key differences in recreational and commercial fishermen. The interactive data with more variables was also made available via ESRI's online mapping services(after signing in).

Based simply off visual inspection, patterns of greating understanding and dissatisfaction of fisheries managment are present amongst commercial fishermen.
